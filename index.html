Welcome to Valley Stream's Community Website

The community would like to thank all Doctors, Nurses, and First-Responders for keeping us safe and healthy, caring and comforting for us, and for put their lives after others. Thank you and we appreciate what you do for all of us!

Valley Stream is a suburban village located in Nassau County, NY. This suburban village, as of 2017, has a population of approximately 40,000 people. It is estimated to hold a median household income of approximately $110,000. The demographics of this suburban village is;

White: 46%
Hispanic: 22%
Black: 17%
Asian: 11%
Other: 4%
