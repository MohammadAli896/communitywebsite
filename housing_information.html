Housing Information
for Valley Stream

There is approximately 12,000 total households in Valley Stream with an average of 3 people per household. There is approximately 5,000 households with children, while there is 7,000 households without children.

There is about 2,000 people below poverty level while there is about 36,000 people above poverty level. There is about 9,000 owner occupied houses and about 3,000 renter occupied houses.

The median housing costs per month is about $2,400.00 and the median house value is about $500,000.00.
