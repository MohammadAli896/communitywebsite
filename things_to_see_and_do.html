Things to See and
Do in Valley Stream

Green Acres Mall

Green Acres Mall is an indoor shopping mall located in Valley Stream, New York, off Sunrise Highway in Nassau County near the border of New York City and the Incorporated Village of Valley Stream. The mall is the 15th largest in the United States. The mall is extremely popular in Nassau County and in the neighboring New York City borough of Queens. The mall is accessible by many Nassau Inter-County Express routes as well as two MTA New York City Bus routes, the Q5 and Q85, that cross the city border. The mall's anchors are JCPenney, Sears, and two Macy's locations. It has all the large brand stores you could need, from clothing to food and house needs. Middle of the range, not too fancy, mall.

Regal Lynbrook and RPX Movie Theater

The sound quality is excellent, the seats fully recline, and the theater is nice and clean. Plenty of space in your seats, which are super comfy and can lean back. The bar has pretty reasonable prices and it's definitely a great option to accompany an evening movie. The staff are always friendly and helpful during visits. The parking can sometimes be a bit inconvenient but the prices arent bad for what you get here.

Five Towns Mini Golf and Batting Range

Long-running seasonal entertainment hub with 18-hole mini-golf, batting cages and target paintball. Make it an all-sports kind of day! Try the mini-golf course, the batting range, the paintball shooting gallery, or the arcade. Keep it moving throughout the night, going from course to course at Five Towns Mini Golf and Batting Range. Bring the whole family to this course, where kiddos are welcomed with open arms. Watch out! Play the paintball field at Five Towns Mini Golf and Batting Range and get ready to run. Forget the hassle of street parking and head to Five Towns Mini Golf and Batting Range for easy access to parking lots. Everyone loves a good game of mini-golf, so take the competition to Five Towns Mini Golf and Batting Range.
